OUR STORY

Precision Since 2018

EdgeFix was founded with a simple mission: to bring professional blade sharpening directly to your doorstep — saving you time, hassle, and money.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and eco-friendly mobile sharpening services that extend the life of your tools and blades, while delivering unmatched convenience and craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in mobile blade repair nationwide — known for precision, integrity, and a commitment to sustainable sharpening practices.

OUR JOURNEY

The EdgeFix Story

2018— Humble Beginnings

EdgeFix started as a single mobile van in Los Angeles, offering on-site knife sharpening for local restaurants and home cooks.

2020— Expanding Services

Added scissors, garden tools, and industrial blade sharpening. Grew to a fleet of 3 mobile units serving the greater metro area.

2023— Statewide Coverage

Expanded operations across the state, partnered with 50+ restaurants, salons, and hardware stores for recurring service.

2026— Industry Leaders

Today, EdgeFix is the premier mobile sharpening service with 5-star ratings, eco-certification, and a commitment to zero-waste sharpening.
