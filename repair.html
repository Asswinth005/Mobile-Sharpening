OUR PROCESS

How Blade Repair Works

1

Inspection & Diagnosis

We assess the damage and provide a free, no-obligation repair estimate.

2

Disassembly (if needed)

Scissors, shears, and multi-tools are carefully disassembled for thorough repair.

3

Precision Repair

Grinding, straightening, rust removal, and edge reprofiling using professional equipment.

4

Sharpening & Finishing

Final edge sharpening, polishing, and protective coating application.

5

Quality Test & Return

Edge test, alignment check, and we deliver back to your doorstep.
